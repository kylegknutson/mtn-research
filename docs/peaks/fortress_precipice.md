## Option A — the Fortress + Precipice combo ⭐

The recommended composed loop (shortest line through both, DEM gain): **~8.7 mi / ~5,200′, Class 3** from the **West Fork Cimarron TH**. The big gain for the modest mileage is the **deep re-climb between them** — this is a steep, loose two-peak day, not a stroll.

**Route order:** West Fork Cimarron TH → **Fortress Pk (13,246')** → drop to the connecting saddle → **Precipice Pk (13,141')** → back to the TH.

> No single recorded track does both (parties tend to do them solo), so the combo here is **composed** from the separate Fortress and Precipice tracks where they share ground. Treat it as a strong-party objective.

---

## Option B — each on its own

**Precipice Pk (solo) — West Fork Cimarron**
The classic approach (e.g. [debravanwinegarden 8/2025](https://debravanwinegarden.blogspot.com/2025/08/precipice-peak-13144-from-west-fork.html)): from the **West Fork Cimarron / Wetterhorn Basin TH (1.9 mi, ~10,760')**, then a **faint, fragmented social trail and an ultra-steep footpath straight up through deep forest** to the divide. Short but brutal and loose — **Class 3**. Few visitors. Roughly **~4–6 mi RT**.

**Fortress Pk (solo) — West Fork Cimarron**
A bit longer (Fortress sits further S toward Coxcomb), **Class 3 on loose Cimarron rock**. Recorded Fortress tracks run **~5–7 mi RT**.

## Conditions / season

- **Best window:** **July–September** — high, remote; steep N-facing approaches hold snow.
- **Terrain:** steep, **loose Class 3** Cimarron rock — careful scrambling, helmet advised; the Precipice approach trail is faint and very steep.
- **Storms:** exposed divide — early start.
- **Cell:** dead — carry an InReach.

## TL;DR

- **Two northern Cimarron 13ers near Coxcomb**, both steep **loose Class 3**.
- **Combo (recommended):** Fortress + Precipice from the **West Fork Cimarron TH** — **~8.7 mi / ~5,200′** (big re-climb between them; composed from the solo tracks). Strong-party day.
- **Solo:** Precipice is short but **ultra-steep + faint trail** (~4–6 mi RT); Fortress ~5–7 mi RT.
- **GMUG NF / Uncompahgre Wilderness**; ~5h30 drive (US-50 → Cimarron). Cell dead — InReach.
