# Shawn's Peak Research

and "unclimbed" computed from [his 14ers checklist](https://www.14ers.com/php14ers/checklist.php?usernum=872&checklist=13ers&rk=ranked&nm=both&climbstatus=all) (352 ranked 13ers done).

Same research engine as the [main site](https://kylegknutson.github.io/mtn-research/) — routes, link-ups, conditions, and trip-report references across 14ers.com + Lists of John + peakbagger, overlaid on CalTopo maps.
